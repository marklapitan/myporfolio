About Me

I, Mark Dela Torre Lapitan (born October 30, 1985), am a Filipino web developer enthusiast. I am a fitness advocate and nature lover who gets fond of driving to explore the beauty of nature. Also, I value my religion and its accompanying beliefs. I was raised in a family of Born Again Christians but later on, embraced Islam and became a revert Muslim in 2010. Since then, I have acquainted myself with continuous learning and active participation in sharing the teachings of Islam whenever the opportunity arrives.

Early Life

I was born on 30 October 1985 in Lubang Island, Municipality of Looc, Occidental Mindoro to Liza Dela Torre and Reymark Lapitan. Having born out of wedlock, I was raised by my grandparents, Salvador Dela Torre and Sile√±a Mendoza who spent most of their time juggling humble various works in the rural area of Tabao to make ends meet. At an early age, I was trained by my grandparents to do different household chores like cooking, fetching water and carrying various loads of stuff which enhanced my independence, muscular fitness and endurance. Despite poor living conditions, I was able to obtain my primary education at Tabao Looc Elementary School in Occidental Mindoro and secondary education at San Jose National High School in Occidental Mindoro and Talavera Nueva Ecija National High School in Nueva Ecija. My passion for physical fitness which stemmed from the early training of my grandfather through our humble living continued during my secondary education until college in the Divine Word College of San Jose, Occidental Mindoro where I became active in Martial Arts and later on became an instructor in a fitness gym situated within the locality of San Jose, Occidental Mindoro. Moreover, to save up funds for my studies, I travelled to Manila and worked as a Merchandiser in the different branches of Puregold at Kalentong, Shaw Boulevard, Cubao and San Juan. Eventually, I went back to San Jose, Occidental Mindoro to continue and finish my college and later on earned a degree in Bachelor of Science in Computer Science in 2010.

Career
